Show Notes

Lazarus and Labyrinth realize their team-up is far from over. Both realize there's something more sinister than the hapless failings of Nick Northcutt. Wanting to understand more about The Book of Unknown Devils, they decide they need more information from the mysterious Lima.

If you want to learn more about the weird world we're playing in - we started a World Anvil page for you!
