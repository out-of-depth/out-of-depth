Jun 6, 2023 Electric Bastionland

Nezbit, Sallie, and Tremolo go on their first job for Mr. Dick, protecting a Mock Fox named Murken. Unfortunately a simple task turns into an explosive situation and our intrepid travelers will have to make some important decisions, all while one of their ally's life hangs on by a thread. Bodies quickly pile up in the wild first episode of HACKWAY HEIGHTS, a twelve part actual play podcast using the Electric Bastionland RPG system.
